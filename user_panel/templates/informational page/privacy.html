Privacy Policy

Effective Date: {% now "F j, Y" %}

At VigiLink, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our neighborhood safety watch system.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Account credentials (username, password)
  • Residential information (address, subdivision, block/lot)
  • Emergency contact information (if provided)
  • Profile pictures (if uploaded)

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our platform:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and settings
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages viewed and features used
  • Actions taken within the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Processing and completing transactions
  • Verifying your identity and eligibility
  • Communicating with you about your account, updates, and security alerts
  • Facilitating community safety reporting and communication
  • Improving and optimizing our platform
  • Protecting against unauthorized access and fraudulent activity

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With designated security personnel within your subdivision (for incident reports)
  • With other residents in your community (for community posts and messages)
  • With service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In connection with a business transfer or acquisition

We will never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Anonymous Reporting

When you submit an anonymous report:

  • Your identity will not be revealed to other residents or security personnel
  • The content of your report will be forwarded to designated security personnel
  • System administrators may have access to report metadata for technical purposes

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Secure data storage practices

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of certain communications
  • Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18 without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
